Templates

Use nodel-template with a native <template> child to render repeated controls from one inert source block. Placeholders are replaced in text and attributes, so action and signal names can be generated consistently. Built-in placeholders include {{name}}, {{number}}, {{index}}, {{repeat}}, and {{item}}, where {{item}} is {{name}}{{number}}. Custom data-* attributes are also available as placeholders, for example data-action-prefix becomes {{actionPrefix}}.

Repeated Controls

The rendered controls become siblings of nodel-template, which keeps them as direct children of the surrounding control grid. 

<nodel-control-grid columns="2" md="4">
  <nodel-template name="Zone" repeat="4" start="1">
    <template>
      <nodel-button join="{{item}}" variant="primary" tone="soft">
        {{name}} {{number}}
        <nodel-status-indicator signal="{{item}}Online" label="{{name}} {{number}} online"></nodel-status-indicator>
      </nodel-button>
    </template>
  </nodel-template>
</nodel-control-grid>

Fader Template
